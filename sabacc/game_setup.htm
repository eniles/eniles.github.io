Real-Life Setup

These are pictures of a real-life setup used by a dealer to track a Sabacc game run in Star Wars Galaxies.

  1. Placemats to keep track of each player's hand and position at the table
  2. Sabacc deck draw and discard piles
  3. Flowchart with marker to keep track of where you are in the round
  4. Pad of paper and calculator to keep track of the hand and sabacc pots and the current bet
  5. Sabacc rules printout and notes
  6. Marker to keep track of the active player
  7. Dealer marker to show which player is the dealer on this hand
